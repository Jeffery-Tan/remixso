And they're all being told the same thing: "You need to be everywhere."

X. LinkedIn. Instagram. Email newsletters. TikTok. YouTube Shorts.

That's six platforms. Six different formats. Six different content languages. For one person with no team and no social media manager.

The result? Most solo creators default to one platform. They leave audience — and revenue — on every other channel. Not because they don't have more to say, but because the "content adaptation tax" is too damn high.

RemixSo fixes this. Paste one article. Pick your platforms. Get platform-optimized posts in seconds — keeping your voice, just changing the format.

Stop rewriting. Start creating.
